About Nodester

Nodester is the one and only 100% open source Node.JS hosting platform-as-a-service (PaaS). Our platform is written in Node.JS and hosted on Amazon EC2 (compliments of Tropo).

Nodester offers free Node.JS hosting with a very simple command line tool; however, Nodester can also be deployed as a private cloud in your own datacenter, public cloud service, or even your own laptop!
